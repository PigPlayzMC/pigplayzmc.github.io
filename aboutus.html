Westcountry Repairs was established in March of 2024 with two goals; to reduce the environmental impact of the tech we use of a daily basis, and to save you money.

We strive daily to deliver great value repairs and get your tech back to work. Every day, we do our best to deliver quality service and value for money. Since establishment, our goals have not shifted from these initial objectives. Still, we aim to help you break through from the toxic 'Purchase -> Breakage -> New purchase' cycle.
